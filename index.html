Some common questions were often asked

How do I choose plants for gifting?

Choose low-maintenance plants like succulents or lucky bamboo for beginners. Orchids or bonsai are great for plant lovers. Pick plants that match the recipient’s space and care ability.

What plant is best for my office desk?

Small, low-light plants like snake plants, pothos, or succulents are perfect for desks. They require minimal care and brighten up your workspace.

How do I select plants for my garden soil type?

Test your soil's pH and texture, then pick plants suited to it. For example, succulents love sandy soil, while loamy soil is great for vegetables and flowers.

How do I know if a plant is safe for pets?

Research pet-safe plants like spider plants and parlor palms. Avoid toxic plants like lilies or pothos if you have cats or dogs.

What are the best plants for purifying indoor air?

Peace lilies, snake plants, and spider plants are great for purifying air. They’re easy to care for and improve air quality at home.

Which plants require minimal care?

Snake plants, pothos, and succulents are low-maintenance options. They thrive with little water and care, making them perfect for busy people.
